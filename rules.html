0 Core Tenets

Presence
Be seen when it matters; an invisible member is a liability.
Discretion
Faction business remains internal; screenshots or leaks trigger removal.
Mutual Aid
Your call is our call; assistance flows both directions without IOUs.

Honour these principles and the Cartel stands behind you unequivocally.

1 Activity Expectations

Daily Presence
Log in once every 24 h; “Last Action” > 24 h flags you inactive.
Event Notifications
Enable push alerts in the Torn app (or e-mail/SMS via Settings → Preferences) so OC and chain pings reach you within seconds.
OC Commitment
Join an OC only if you can stay active for its 3–6-day timer and the sub-1-minute execution.
Chain Windows
Opt-in hitters respond in < 5 min; keep 50 + Energy ready, fast targets bookmarked, hospital time < 10 min.
Vacation Mode
Away ≥ 72 h? Toggle it and post an ETA; no notice → removal after 3 days idle.
Quick Response
Reply to urgent leadership mails or Discord pings so rosters adjust in real time.

2 Organised Crimes 2.0

Scenario System
Every OC is now a named scenario (Lv 1-9). Four difficulty tiers use Greek symbols:
Λ Lambda (1-2, cost 1 Scope) • Σ Sigma (3-5, 2) • Ψ Psi (6-8, 4) • Ω Omega (9+, 12).
Scope Resource
Factions start with 25 Scope, cap 100, and regenerate +1/day. Completing scenarios also awards extra Scope.
Planning → Execution
After the day-countdown, a sub-1-minute execution fires. All members must be in Torn, healthy and carrying the required items or the run fails.
Roles & Items
Each participant is auto-assigned a role (Bomber, Techie, Muscle). Materials are consumed (C-4, Zip Ties); Tools persist (Jemmy, DSLR). Check the scenario panel before joining.
Payout System
On success, leadership chooses a 0-100 % split between faction and members. Cash can hit wallets or faction bank; item loot is distributed randomly or liquidated and split.
Readiness & Contingencies
Join only if you can stay active 3-6 days and keep “Last Action” < 24 h. Can’t log in? Ping leadership immediately — one missing member can void days of planning.

3 External Conduct

Faction Relations Tags
Leaders control our status with other factions (Friendly / Neutral / Hostile). Do not provoke, negotiate, or promise favours on your own.
Target Clearance
Approval required before hospital sprees, territory raids, bounties > $1 000 000, or chain hits on ranked allies. One rogue attack can wipe perks and invite Ω-tier retaliation.
No Outside “Help”
Hiring mercs or intimidation squads without consent counts as hostile action and triggers removal.
Trade & Marketplace
Use Torn’s Trade window / Item Market; screenshot deals > $50 m. No RMT or crypto — Torn bans both parties.
Chat / Forums / Global
Follow the 2025 Community Rules: no slurs, doxxing, or IRL threats. Keep internal strategy off public channels; mark jokes clearly to avoid misfires.
Harassment & Intimidation
Banter is fine; stalking, spam PMs, or strong-arm tactics are not. Torn logs all messages.
Scamming
Zero-scam policy. Your offer = a contract, even if the game allows otherwise.

DISCLAIMER — ZERO TOLERANCE: Members who act externally without consulting leadership will be expelled swiftly.

4 Internal Culture

Mentoring
New arrivals can request a mentor for builds and revenue routes.
Resource Pool
Donate spare meds, ammo, or job points to the armoury; what helps one helps all.
Knowledge Sharing
Post patch-note finds and economy tips; smart play keeps profits high.

5 Warfare Risks

Non-Warring Stance
We prioritise profit and preservation over conquest.
Energy Drain
Random hits waste energy better spent on OCs and gym gains.
Perk Protection
Specials (travel slots, armoury, rehab discount) vanish if respect is wiped.
Defensive Hits
When leaders call a shield wall, one timely revive or defend can end hostilities.
