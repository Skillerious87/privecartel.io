0 Core Tenets

Presence
Be seen when it matters; an invisible member is a liability.
Discretion
Faction business remains internal; screenshots or leaks trigger removal.
Mutual Aid
Your call is our call; assistance flows both directions without IOUs.

Honour these principles and the Cartel stands behind you unequivocally.

1 Activity Expectations

Daily Presence
Log in at least once every 24 h; “Last Action” > 24 h flags you as inactive.
Event Notifications
Enable push alerts in the Torn mobile app (or e-mail/SMS via Settings → Preferences) so OC and chain pings reach you within seconds.
OC Commitment
If you join an OC 2.0 scenario, remain active for its full 3–6 day timer and the sub-1-minute execution phase. Missing the execution voids the run and burns Scope.
Chain Windows
Only members who opt in must respond in < 5 min during scheduled windows. Keep 50+ Energy ready, fast targets bookmarked, and hospital time < 10 min.
Vacation Mode
Away for ≥ 72 h? Toggle Vacation Mode in Torn settings and post an ETA in our faction thread. Slots are protected; no notice → removal after 3 days idle.
Quick Response
Leaders may send urgent in-game mail or Discord @pings; reply when possible so rosters adjust in real time.

2 Organised Crimes 2.0

Scenario System
Every OC is now a named scenario (Lv 1–9). Four difficulty tiers use the Greek symbols:
Λ Lambda (Lv 1–2, cost 1 Scope) • Σ Sigma (3–5, cost 2) • Ψ Psi (6–8, cost 4) • Ω Omega (9+, cost 12).
Scope Resource
Factions start with 25 Scope (cap 100) and regenerate +1/day. Completing scenarios also awards extra Scope.
Planning → Execution
After the day-countdown, a sub-1-minute execution fires. All members must be in Torn, healthy, and carrying the required items or the run fails.
Roles & Items
Every participant is auto-assigned a role (e.g., Bomber, Techie, Muscle). Materials (C-4, zip ties) are consumed; tools (Jemmy, DSLR) persist. Check the scenario panel or ask leadership for the exact shopping list.
Payout System
On success, leadership chooses a 0–100 % split between faction and members. Cash can hit member wallets or the faction bank; item loot is distributed randomly or sold at market value.
Readiness & Contingencies
Join only if you can stay active the full 3–6 days and keep “Last Action” < 24 h. Can’t log in? Ping a leader immediately—one missing member can void days of planning.

3 External Conduct

Your actions outside the faction reflect on all of us. Follow these rules when dealing with other factions, markets, and public channels.

Faction Relations Tags
Leaders control our status with other factions (Friendly / Neutral / Hostile). Do not provoke, negotiate, or promise favors on your own.
Target Clearance
Obtain approval before hospital sprees, territory raids, bounties > $1 000 000, or chain hits on ranked allies. One rogue attack can wipe perks and invite Ω-tier retaliation.
No Outside “Help”
Arranging mercenaries or intimidation squads without leadership consent is forbidden. Recruiting outsiders—or threatening to—counts as hostile action and will trigger removal.
Trade & Marketplace
Use Torn’s Trade window / Item Market; screenshot deals > $50 m. No real-money, cross-game, or crypto trades—Torn bans both parties.
Chat / Forums / Global
Follow the 2025 Community Rules: no slurs, doxxing, or IRL threats. Keep internal strategy off public channels; mark jokes clearly to avoid misfires.
Harassment & Intimidation
Light banter is fine; stalking, spam PMs, or strong-arm tactics are not. All messages are logged by Torn and reviewed on complaint.
Scamming
Faction-wide zero-scam policy. Your offer = a contract, even if the game allows otherwise.

DISCLAIMER — ZERO TOLERANCE: Members who act externally without consulting leadership will be expelled swiftly.

4 Internal Culture

We build each other up through guidance, generosity, and shared knowledge. Lean in, ask questions, and give back.

Mentoring
New arrivals can request a mentor for builds, routes, and revenue strategies.
Resource Pool
Donate spare meds, ammo, or job points to the armoury—what helps one helps all.
Knowledge Sharing
Post patch-note finds, economy tips, and smart-play tricks so the entire faction stays ahead.

5 Warfare Risks

Non-Warring Stance
We prioritise profit and preservation over conquest.
Energy Drain
Random hits waste energy better spent on OCs and gym gains.
Perk Protection
Specials (travel slots, armoury, rehab discount) vanish if respect is wiped.
Defensive Hits
When leaders call a shield wall, one timely revive or defend can end hostilities.
