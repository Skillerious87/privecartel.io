Basics

Should I rent a Private Island?

Yes ‑ Rent a PI with an airstrip ASAP.

Why the Airstrip matters

  • 30 % faster flights after the 2025 cooldown nerf.
  • +10 energy/h & +6 400 happy – roughly one extra Xanax every 18 h.
  • +10 carry slots – doubles profit on plushie/flower loops.

Smart path

  • Rent (≈ $3 m/day) while you farm travel profit.
  • Buy once you have 60 days of rent banked or stable 6 m+/day income.
  • Upgrade Sea Dock last – Airstrip is the MVP.
Fast cash & hitting level 15 quickly

1 • Power‑level to 15

  • Follow Balder’s levelling guide for an optimised task list.
  • Spam Shoplifting → Convenience Store (Crime 2.0) at ≥ 90 % success for rapid XP.
  • Finish all starter missions (≈ 750 k cash + merits + XP bursts).
  • Join Army job early & dump 250 points into instant Str/Def boost.
  • Spin Leslie’s Wheel daily – occasional 50 k‑200 k XP jackpots.

2 • Travel‑trading from level 15

  • Route: Mexico → Canada → Japan (best profit/token mix).
  • Large Suitcase + faction Logistics perk = 120‑token capacity.
  • Average margin: $35‑45 k per plushie/flower → ~$5 m per loop.

3 • Layer faction income

  • Ranked OCs: Tier‑3 nets $6 m+ split 10 ways.
  • 3★ Oil Rig territory: $1.2 m/day straight to members.
  • Ranked Chains: tokens redeem for Donator Packs, meds, points.

Milestone: $200 m net‑worth + PI rent achievable inside 30 days of active trading.

Checkpoint % for tier‑8/9 OCs

Benchmarks

  • Working stats: ≥ 20 m (TV → Logistics starter path).
  • Battle stats: ≥ 150 m (Jug + Xanax routine).
  • Crime successes: ≥ 5 k (see nerve grind below).

Boost success rate

  • Farm Parcel Delivery (Theft Tier 4) – 95 % success, best Crime XP/min.
  • Faction specials Mastermind & Precision Planning = +16 % pass chance.
  • Keep Keycard, Det‑Cord, EMP on hand – auto‑slot into scenario.
  • Be home & healthy when OC executes – travellers/hospitals auto‑fail.

Training & Stats

Booster stack for max gym gains

Daily cycle

  • Xanax every 8 h (250 E).
  • Nova Protein Jug (+30 % gains for 15 min).
  • Quick Chocolate Cake for +5 k happy jump.
  • Train in Frontline Fitness or higher‑tier gym.

Weekly routine

  • Rehab after 6 Xanax (≈ $3 m) – resets addiction & refills happy.
  • Time big sessions with faction Steadfast 10 (+10 % gains).
  • Use book “Finding the Gym Rat” every 30 days for +25 % gains.

Expect ~8‑10 m stats/day at 50 m bracket; scales higher with RAMP stock & advanced gyms.

Stat ratio after dodge soft‑cap

Road map

  • 0‑50 m each: 1 Str : 2 Agi : 2 Def
  • 50‑500 m: flatten to 1 : 1 : 1
  • 500 m +: specialise – Str for chain hitters, Def for tanks.

Keep Speed minimal until you chase billions.

When am I chain‑ready?

Checklist

  • ≥ 25 m total stats with fully repaired gear.
  • 10 med‑kits + 3 Xanax banked.
  • Time for five 5‑hit minis or one 100‑hit brick.

Ranked Chains cap at 20 hitters – even mid‑tier minis add respect multipliers.

Is Donator status worth it in 2025?

Why it’s unbeatable ROI

  • +240 energy/day – equal to one free Xanax every 30 h.
  • 150 E cap – overfills to 300 E when married.
  • Faster happy regen, bazaar access, no chat delay, free stat graphs, extra merits.

How to afford it

  • Buy Donator Pack (~$60 m) – one plushie loop covers half.
  • Or subscribe ($5/month) – gets 90 points, tradeable for $400‑500 m.

Activate as soon as possible – daily energy advantage compounds forever.
