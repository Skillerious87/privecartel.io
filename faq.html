Faction FAQ

Everything you need to thrive in Torn – July 2025, Crime 2.0.

Basics

Should I rent a Private Island?

Yes – Rent a PI with an airstrip ASAP.

Why an airstrip?

  • 30 % faster flights – offsets 2025 cooldown increase.
  • +10 Energy/hour & +6 400 Happy – roughly an extra Xanax every 18 h.
  • +10 carry slots – doubles profit on plushie/flower loops.

Smart progression

  • Rent first (≈ $3 m/day) while you build travel income.
  • Purchase once you have ~60 days of rent banked.
  • Add other upgrades (Sea Dock, Spa) later – Airstrip is priority.
Fast cash & reaching Level 15

Step 1 – Power‑level to 15

  • Follow Balder’s levelling guide (task order & tips).
  • Spam Shoplifting – Convenience Store at ≥ 90 % success for quick experience.
  • Finish all starter missions (≈ $750 000 & 3 merits).
  • Join the Army, spend 250 job points on Strength and Defense boosts.
  • Spin Leslie’s Wheel daily – XP jackpots accelerate levels.

Step 2 – Travel‑trading from Level 15

  • Route: Mexico → Canada → Japan.
  • Use a Large Suitcase (120 tokens) + Logistics perk for max capacity.
  • Profit target: ~ $35‑45 k profit per token → ~$5 m per round trip.

Step 3 – Layer faction income

  • Join every Ranked Organized Crime – Tier 3 pays ~$6 m split 10 ways.
  • Capture or hold a 3★ Illegal Casino or Point Broker racket for major passive rewards.
  • Participate in faction chains for bonus respect & credits.

Goal: Achieving ~$200 m net‑worth and PI rent within 30 days is realistic for an active trader.

Improving OC checkpoint success

Benchmarks for Tier 8/9 Organized Crimes

  • Working Stats: 20 million+ (TV → Logistics job path).
  • Battle Stats: 150 million+ total.
  • Crime Successes: 5 000+ (see nerve‑grind FAQ below).

Boosting success chance

  • Faction specials Mastermind and Precision Planning add +16 % combined success.
  • Carry required tools (Keycard, Detonating Cord, EMP Device) to auto‑fill roles.
  • Ensure you are in Torn, not travelling or hospitalized, when the OC executes.

Training & Stats

Efficient gym‑gain routine

Daily cycle

  • Take Xanax every 8 hours (250 Energy).
  • Consume a Sports Drink (+25 Energy) if available.
  • Top up Happy (Chocolate Cake, Wine) before training for bonus gains.
  • Train all energy in Frontline Fitness or higher unlocked gym.

Weekly maintenance

  • Visit Rehab after ~6 Xanax to reset addiction & refill Happy.
  • Align heavy training with faction Steadfast bonus (+10 % gains if unlocked).
  • Complete the “Sports Science” education block early for +10 % gym gains.
Stat training roadmap

0 – 50 million in each stat

Build a solid foundation:
1 Strength : 2 Dexterity : 2 Defense while keeping Speed minimal. Dexterity (dodge) and Defense (damage reduction) make early fights easier.

50 – 500 million range

Equalize to a balanced 1 : 1 : 1 : 1 across all four stats. Bringing Speed in line improves hit rate; balanced stats prevent glaring weaknesses.

500 million +

Specialize based on role:

  • Chain hitter → Strength focus for faster knock‑outs.
  • Defensive tank → Defense focus to survive longer on walls.
  • Maintain Dexterity for dodge and Speed for accuracy; no stat should be ignored completely.

Speed may remain the lowest priority until you reach billions in other stats.

When am I chain‑ready?

Any level can help a chain – every hit adds respect.

How to contribute effectively

  • Create a target list of players you can beat (use spy reports or tools like Torntargets.com).
  • Prioritize the highest‑level opponents you can reliably defeat – higher level = more respect.
  • Have at least 10 medical items and a few Xanax ready for the chain window.
  • Coordinate with factionmates – even five single‑hit wins can push a chain to bonus thresholds.

Respect earned is proportional to the target’s level, so smart target selection matters more than your own level.

First stock benefits to unlock

Recommended purchase order

  2. Phalanx Tactical Solutions (PTK) – +2 Energy/hour passive.
  3. Torn City Bank (TCB) – doubles long‑term bank interest.

Tips

Is a PI Airship Dock worth it?

Essential if you average four or more flights per day.

  • Stacks with Airstrip for a total ~55 % flight cooldown reduction.
  • +8 item slots – enables 50‑plushie China runs (~$30 m profit).
  • Break‑even around 45 days for active traders.
Key education courses to finish first

Fast‑track course list

  1. Sports Science (Physical Education) – +10 % gym gains.
  2. Intravenous Therapy (Biology) – unlock Blood Bags +15 % medical potency.
  3. Bachelor of Business Management – +10 % melee damage and job‑point merit.
  4. Ammo Conservation (Mathematics) – 20 % ammunition savings.
  5. Cyber Security Diploma – reduces crime detection chance.

Speed hacks

  • Combine 20 % education merits, 10 % Principal job bonus, and 10 % WSU stock for 40 % faster courses.
  • Use Hair Salon (7★) job points on long final exams.

Warfare & Advanced

Budget combat load‑out

Starter gear (≤ $15 m)

  • AK‑47 (Mexico) with Stability and High‑Capacity Magazine.
  • Crowbar for stun; upgrade to Katana when funds allow.
  • Kevlar Vest and Military Helmet for 12 % damage reduction.
  • Carry three Flashbang Grenades to lower enemy accuracy.

Upgrade path

  • Steyr AUG at ~250 million stats for better accuracy scaling.
  • Upgrade armor to SWAT set, then Dragonskin Vest + Composite Helmet.
  • Save weapon shards for Elite modifications above 500 million stats.
High‑value faction territory blocks

The most lucrative rackets (mobile‑friendly list):

  • Illegal Casino (5 levels) – Up to $400 million cash per day at level 5.
  • Point Broker (5 levels) – Up to 8 000 Torn Points daily (convertible to billions).
  • Money Launderer (5 levels) – Up to $160 million cash daily.
  • Protection Racket (5 levels) – Up to $80 million cash daily.
  • Street Surgeon / Drug Lab – Provides Blood Bags or drugs; useful for faction warfare supplies.
  • Truck Stop – Energy cans (10–160 daily) for global training pushes.

Central‑city blocks tend to spawn higher‑level rackets more often. Prioritize & defend these blocks for long‑term passive income.

Extras

Efficient nerve‑bar growth

Crime 2.0 grind loop

  • Maintain 400‑crime streaks for a +25 % experience multiplier.
  • Use drugs that grant nerve (e.g. Cannabis for +8–12, LSD for +5) during long farming sessions.
  • Rehab regularly to offset drug addiction and restore Happy, which speeds nerve regeneration.

Consistent grinding yields roughly one new nerve bar point every 4‑5 days.

Your high‑efficiency daily routine
  • Energy & Nerve: Spend on gym and crimes every login.
  • Travel chain: Book next flight before landing to keep cooldown rolling.
  • Faction: Join Organized Crimes and chain hits as slots open.
  • Education: Always keep a course queued.
  • Bank sweep: Deposit spare cash into 31‑day term weekly.
  • Happy care: Wine + Plush Bear daily; rehab after six Xanax.
Is Donator status worth it?

Benefits

  • +240 Energy/day (720 E vs 480 E) – equivalent to one free Xanax every 30 h.
  • Energy cap 150 (300 with marriage overfill).
  • Faster Happy regen, personal bazaar, no chat delay, stat graphs, extra merits.

Funding options

  • Buy a Donator Pack (~ $23 million).
  • Monthly subscription ($5) → 90 points, tradeable if you need cash.

Best long‑term return on investment in Torn – activate as soon as possible.
