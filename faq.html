Basics

Should I rent a Private Island?

Yes – Rent a PI with an airstrip ASAP.

Why an airstrip?

  • 30 % faster flights – offsets cooldown changes.
  • +10 Energy/hour & +6 400 Happy – ~1 extra Xanax every 18 h.
  • +10 carry slots – doubles travel‑trade profit.

Smart progression

  • Rent first (≈ $3 m/day) while building travel income.
  • Buy once you have ~60 days of rent banked.
  • Other upgrades (Sea Dock, Spa) later—Airstrip first.
Fast cash & reaching Level 15

Step 1 – Power‑level to 15

  • Follow Balder’s levelling guide.
  • Spam Shoplifting – Convenience Store at ≥ 90 % success for rapid XP.
  • Finish starter missions (~$750 k & 3 merits).
  • Army job: spend 250 points on Strength & Defense boosts.
  • Spin Leslie’s Wheel for random XP bursts daily.

Step 2 – Travel trading from Level 15

  • Route: Mexico → Canada → Japan.
  • Large Suitcase + Logistics = 120 tokens.
  • Aim for ~$5 m profit per loop (≈ $35–45 k/token).

Step 3 – Layer faction income

  • Join Ranked OCs—Tier 3 pays ~$6 m split 10 ways.
  • Fight for a 3★ Illegal Casino or Point Broker racket.
  • Chain for respect—tokens convert to packs/points.
Improving OC checkpoint success

Benchmarks for Tier 8/9 OCs

  • Working Stats: 20 million + (TV → Logistics).
  • Battle Stats: 150 million + total.
  • Crime Successes: 5 000 + successful crimes.

Boost success rate

  • Activate faction specials Mastermind & Precision Planning (+16 %).
  • Carry Keycard, Detonating Cord & EMP Device to auto‑fill.
  • Stay in Torn & above 25 % life when the OC fires.

Training & Stats

Efficient gym‑gain routine

Daily cycle

  • Xanax every 8 h (250 Energy).
  • Optional Sports Drink for +25 E top‑up.
  • Boost Happy (Chocolate Cake + Wine) before training.
  • Spend all Energy in Frontline Fitness or better.

Weekly

  • Rehab after ~6 Xanax to reset addiction.
  • Time heavy sessions with faction Steadfast (+10 % gains).
  • Complete Sports Science course early for +10 % gains.
Stat training roadmap

0 – 50 million each

1 Strength : 2 Dexterity : 2 Defense; keep Speed low.

50 – 500 million

Flatten to 1 : 1 : 1 : 1 for a balanced profile.

500 million +

  • Chain hitter → focus Strength.
  • Tank / wall defender → focus Defense.

Speed can be last until you’re deep into the billions.

Ready for the Chain? — Everything you need to know

1. Mindset

Every member matters. No minimum level: if you can win fights you can chain. A single hit adds respect; 5–10 hits can tip a milestone.

2. Respect formula (2025)

  • Target level drives base respect (higher level = higher respect).
  • Your level/stats no longer reduce respect, but losing the fight gives 0.
  • Chaining milestones (25‑hit, 50‑hit, 100‑hit…) multiply earned respect—collectively huge.

3. Building a target list

  • Use Duke mission targets, faction intel and previous chain logs to bookmark foes with useful respect and beatable stats.
  • Refresh the list monthly—player stats change.
  • Carry multiple load‑outs (stun melee, tear‑gas, etc.) to handle different defences.

4. Gear & meds checklist

  • 10 Medical items (First‑Aid Kits, Blood Bags) + 3 Xanax pre‑chain.
  • Full weapon repairs; ensure ammo stock.
  • Optional boosters: Box of Grenades, Energy Drinks for rapid re‑engage.

5. Timing and etiquette

  • Stay in city (not travelling) during chain window.
  • Use faction chat/Discord to avoid double‑tapping same target.
  • During “end push” aim for quick wins—ignore hospital time; speed matters.

6. After‑action

  • Log hits/KO times—helps leadership fine‑tune future chains.
  • Donate spare meds to armory for next chain.
  • Spend chain tokens on Donator Packs or boosters to compound gains.

Essential if you fly ≥ 4 times/day.

  • Stacks with Airstrip → ~55 % cooldown cut.
  • +8 slots = 50‑plushie China run (~$30 m profit).
  • ROI ≈ 45 days.

Extras

Growing your nerve bar

Crime 2.0 loop

  • Maintain 400‑crime streak for +25 % EXP bonus.
  • Use Cannabis (+8–12 nerve) or LSD (+5 nerve) for big refill windows.
  • Rehab every 10–12 drug uses to keep Happy regen fast.

Average gain: ~1 new nerve point every 4–5 days with perfect uptime.

High‑efficiency daily checklist
  • Energy & Nerve: Spend at every login.
  • Travel: Book return flight on landing.
  • Faction: Fill OC slots; chain if live.
  • Education: Queue next course instantly.
  • Bank: Sweep spare cash weekly.
  • Happy: Wine + Plush Bear daily, rehab after 6 Xanax.
Is Donator status worth it?

Benefits

  • +240 Energy/day (720 E vs 480 E).
  • Energy cap 150 (300 w/ marriage overfill).
  • Faster Happy regen, personal bazaar, extra merits.

Funding

  • Donator Pack (~$23 m).
  • $5 monthly sub → 90 points (resell if broke).

Activate early—energy advantage compounds for life.
