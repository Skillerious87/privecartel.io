Torn Targets Guide

Step-by-step to find the best low-risk chain targets and leveling spots on TornTargets.com.

Back to Guides

1. What Is Torn Targets?

TornTargets.com is a community-driven tool that finds optimal attack targets in Torn:

  • Pinpoint low-level players for fast XP and respect gains.
  • Filter opponents by estimated profit, hospital chance, travel time, and more.
  • Save and recall your custom searches instantly.

2. Obtaining Your Torn API Key

  1. Log in at torn.com.
  2. Click your username (top-right) → select Preferences.
  3. In the left sidebar click Settings → API Keys.
  4. Under “My Keys” click Generate New Key.
  5. In the dialog:
    • Give it a descriptive name (e.g. “Privé Targets”).
    • Under Selections, check Basic (for faction info) and any other needed data.
    • Click Create, then Copy Key.
  6. Paste the key into Torn Targets when prompted. Keep it secret—it grants access to your faction data.
  7. You can revoke or regenerate this key anytime from the same API Keys page.

3. Logging In to Torn Targets

1. Visit TornTargets.com.
2. Paste your API key into the top-right field.
3. Click Connect—your username will appear when successful.

4. Configure Your Search

Customize your criteria below. Then click Search for updated targets.

Parameter Description Example
Min Level Exclude players below this level. 1
Max Level Exclude players above this level. 15
Min Profit ($) Only targets with estimated net profit per hit ≥ this. 1,000,000
Max Hospital Chance (%) Skip targets with too high a hospital risk. 20
Sort By Select the metric to order results by. Profit/Hit

5. Reading Your Results

  • Name — click to open Torn profile.
  • Level & Respect — quick glance stats.
  • Profit — estimated net funds per hit.
  • Hospital % — chance of losing time.
  • Travel Time — distance from you.

6. Pro Tips

  • Save filters as bookmarks for instant recall.
  • Maintain 50+ Energy to avoid mid-chain gaps.
  • Rotate target lists every 15-20 hits to dodge revives.
  • Combine with faction chains for maximum respect haul.
